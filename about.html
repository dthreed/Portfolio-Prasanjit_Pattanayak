About Dr. Prasanjit Pattanayak

Dedicated to Excellence in Surgical Oncology

Professional Introduction

Dr. Prasanjit Pattanayak is a highly motivated surgical oncologist with comprehensive training in oncologic surgical procedures and a strong foundation in general surgery. His commitment to patient-centered care and evidence-based practice drives his approach to cancer treatment.

With specialized training from the prestigious Acharya Harihar Post Graduate Institute of Cancer (AHPGIC), Cuttack, and SCB Medical College, Dr. Pattanayak brings extensive expertise in managing a wide spectrum of oncological conditions. His practice emphasizes minimally invasive techniques, comprehensive preoperative assessment, and meticulous postoperative care.

Philosophy of Care

Dr. Pattanayak believes in a holistic approach to cancer treatment that combines surgical excellence with compassionate patient care. He is dedicated to staying current with the latest advances in surgical oncology through continued professional development, research participation, and academic contributions.

His areas of special interest include colorectal cancer surgery, breast cancer surgery, head and neck oncology, and minimally invasive oncologic procedures. Dr. Pattanayak works closely with multidisciplinary teams to ensure comprehensive cancer care that addresses not only the disease but also the overall well-being of his patients.
